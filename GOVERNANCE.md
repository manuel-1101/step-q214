# STEP-Q214 Governance

Maintained by holydraft

---

## 1. Purpose

This document defines the governance structure of the STEP-Q214 specification.

Its purpose is to ensure transparent, consistent, and sustainable development.

---

## 2. Governance Model

STEP-Q214 follows a maintainer-led governance model.

Strategic and technical decisions are made by designated maintainers,
with input from the community.

---

## 3. Maintainers

The following organization is responsible for the project:

- holydraft (manupa GbR)

Primary maintainer:

- Manuel Scholz

Maintainers are responsible for:

- Specification integrity
- Release management
- Review processes
- Conflict resolution
- External representation

---

## 4. Roles

### 4.1 Maintainer

Maintainers have authority to:

- Approve and merge pull requests
- Define releases
- Interpret the specification
- Enforce governance rules

---

### 4.2 Contributors

Contributors may:

- Propose changes
- Submit pull requests
- Participate in discussions
- Provide feedback

Contributors have no decision authority.

---

### 4.3 Advisory Participants (Optional)

Industry partners, platform operators, or research institutions
may be invited as advisory participants.

Advisory participants have no binding voting rights.

---

## 5. Decision Process

### 5.1 Standard Decisions

Most decisions are made by consensus among maintainers.

If consensus cannot be reached, the primary maintainer decides.

---

### 5.2 Major Decisions

Major decisions include:

- Breaking changes
- Governance changes
- Licensing changes
- Long-term roadmap shifts

These require explicit approval by the primary maintainer.

---

## 6. Conflict Resolution

Technical disputes shall be resolved by:

1. Technical discussion
2. Written rationale
3. Maintainer review
4. Final decision

Personal conflicts are not tolerated.

---

## 7. Transparency

All major decisions shall be documented in:

- Pull request discussions
- Release notes
- Governance updates

Closed-door decision making is avoided.

---

## 8. Succession and Continuity

If the primary maintainer becomes unavailable,
holydraft shall designate a successor.

In case holydraft ceases operation,
maintainership may be transferred to a neutral organization.

---

## 9. Amendments

Changes to this governance document require:

- Public proposal
- Maintainer review
- Formal approval

Governance changes are versioned.

---

## 10. External Relations

Maintainers represent STEP-Q214 in:

- Industry partnerships
- Research projects
- Standardization initiatives

No external party may claim official representation without approval.

---

## 11. Legal Responsibility

holydraft is responsible for legal and administrative matters
related to STEP-Q214.
